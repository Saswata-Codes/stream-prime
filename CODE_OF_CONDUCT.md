# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Stream Prime welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still asking clear questions.
- Give feedback that is specific and actionable.
- Respect privacy and never ask people to share stream keys, credentials, or private URLs publicly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Publishing private information without permission
- Sharing malicious code or intentionally harmful instructions
- Derailing issues or pull requests with unrelated arguments

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Open a private security/advisory channel if the report includes private or sensitive information. For general conduct concerns, contact the project maintainer through GitHub.
